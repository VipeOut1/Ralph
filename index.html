Wreck-It Ralph

About Ralph

Wreck-It Ralph" is a fun animated movie about a video game bad guy named Ralph who wants to be a hero for a change. He travels through different arcade games and makes a friend named Vanellope. They team up to prove that anyone can be a hero, no matter their role. The movie is full of adventure, humor, and friendship, and it shows that everyone has the power to change their story.
